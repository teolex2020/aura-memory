## Plan 06.2-04: EpistemicRuntime Refactoring — Remove maintain(), Add Inspection Methods

### What was built

Refactored `@aura/epistemic-runtime` EpistemicRuntimeImpl to align with the new `EpistemicRuntime.Interface` (namespace pattern, 18 inspection methods). Removed `maintain()` entirely (migrated to MaintenanceService in Plan 06). Added constructor with ConceptSurfaceMode + 5 Ref telemetry counters. Implemented 12 simple inspection methods with full filtering, sorting, clamping, and limit logic. EpistemicRuntimeLive is now `Layer.effect` (creates Refs, injects engine services).
